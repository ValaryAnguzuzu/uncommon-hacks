# Project Story / About the project

## Inspiration

_Sink or Swim_ was inspired by a dramatized version of 
the experiences that many CS students have when applying to jobs.
Commonly, one will have to apply to multiple jobs before hearing anything back
(even a rejection).
Oftentimes, we will also be pressed for time, as we may have other goals
or responsibilities that demand our attention, beyond just applying to jobs.
In an effort to provide a somewhat cathartic release, we came up with 
the idea to gamify the job application process.

## What it does

_Sink or Swim_ is a game that presents you a computer-like interface 
where you complete different tasks (skill building via projects, interview prep, etc) to advance you toward your goal of getting a job offer before 
you graduate.
At the same time, you must find time to work so that you can weather your expenses and no go into the red before you get that dream job.

## How we built it

We built _Sink or Swim_ using the Godot game engine and Codex for rapid prototyping.
